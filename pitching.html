PITCHING COMPETITION


What is it?


Pitching is the art of expressing your product in a way that makes people believe in your product. Hence, an entrepreneur needs this at various stages of their career. Hence to give a good understanding of this skill to the students and aspiring entrepreneurs, we're organizing a pitching competition as a pre-event in E-Summit 2k21. For this, the students will pitch for their product/idea before the audience and get guidance regarding their strengths and weaknesses.

The aim of this competition is to impart a good understanding of the skill of pitching in the participants and solve their doubts and queries regarding the same.

Details of the competition:


> The competition will consist of a single round which will take place on 26th of January.

> The participants will be given a product on the same day for which they will have to pitch.

> The participants will be given 5 minutes of preparation time.

Date: 26th January
