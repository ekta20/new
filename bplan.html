B-PLAN COMPETITION


What is it?


B-Plan competition is the flagship event of E-Summit 2k21.
The competition will take place in two stages; the first stage wherein participants have to submit a detailed ppt
and a business model canvas of the of their idea/startup.
In the second stage which will take place on the main days of E-Summit i.e. 30th and 31st January, the participants
will have to pitch their ideas before the judges, fellow competitors and the live audience.
Due to its unique design, B-Plan competitions tests out some of the most important entrepreneurial skills in the
participants and help them get a deep understanding and a hands-on-experience of this field.

Details of each round:


Round 1:


Participants will have to submit the following:


  • A Ppt emphasising on different aspects of their idea/startup like problem statement, target audience,
    solution, competitor analysis etc. (Sample ppt link )
  • A Business model canvas for their idea/startup (Sample business model canvas link)

Submission Date: 24th January


(The top 10 teams from round one will be selected for round two.)


Round 2:


Participants will have to pitch their startup idea before judges, fellow competitors and the audience present.


Date: 30, 31 January
