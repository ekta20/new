BUILD YOUR BRAND!


What is it?


Branding is one of the most essential skills that an entrepreneur should possess. It is so because it is branding which decides how users will perceive a particular product, with what values/feelings does the user associate your product. Hence, to impart a good understanding of this essential concept in the students and the aspiring entrepreneurs, we are organising a branding competition as a pre-event of E-Summit 2k21.

The aim of this pre-event will be to introduce the students with the concept of branding, give them a hands on experience and solve their queries and doubts regarding the same.

Details of the competition:


> The competition will take place from 22-24 January.

> The participants will have to work on branding of their dream company/startup.

> For the same the participants will have to submit following four branding elements till 24 January, 4:00 pm:



  • Logo
  • Tagline
  • Colour Palette
  • Website/App

Date: 22-24 January


The branding elements should be submitted by 24th January, 4pm.
